The Humpback is an Attack class submarine.

Notable Features

The Humpback is one of the fastest Submarines that has full fabrication facilities.

The Humpback is notable for its unbalanced ballasts, and a terminal controlled Battery array.

The door in the front ballast can be left opened for faster descent speed.

The medical bay features both a Medical Fabricator and a Toxin Cabinet.
