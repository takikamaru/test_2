Notable Features

The Orca is notable for its maneuverability, Electrical Discharge Coil, Coilguns, and Reactor. The Orca's Discharge Coil is able to shock nearly the entire ventral side of the submarine. The Orca's Coilguns are able to converge on a target above the fore side. The Reactor is notable for consuming fuel 50% faster than other reactors, and for its short fire threshold (10 seconds).

The Orca's medical bay contains both a Medical Fabricator and a Toxin Cabinet.

The Orca is the most overall maneuverable submarine with full fabrication facilities.
